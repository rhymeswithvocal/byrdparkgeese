Our Goal

Richmond's 311 service handles thousands of service requests per month, but a city resident may not know what's happening in their own neighborhood. Our 311 responsive text service allows anybody–no app or smartphone required–to reach the 311 database via text and learn the status of nearby work, or submit a new service request without calling in.

Project Brief

Richmond's 311 service handles thousands of resident requests — but follow-up communication is slow, manual, and easy to miss. We built a lightweight system that sends proactive SMS and email updates to residents as their 311 cases move through the city's workflow, so nobody is left wondering what happened to their pothole report.

Our Goal

Richmond's 311 service handles thousands of service requests per month, but a city resident may not know what's happening in their own neighborhood. Our 311 responsive text service allows anybody, in any language, with any phone (no app or smartphone required) to reach the 311 database via text. They can learn the status of nearby work or submit a new service request without calling in.

Try It!

  1. Send a text to 804-244-9471 that includes a location in Richmond! Could be your house, a pothole you know and love, or one of the suggested texts below. Addresses do not need to be perfectly formatted in the text!
    • There's a pothole at Kensington and Cleveland.
    • I see graffiti in the VMFA garden.
    • The sidewalk is broken in front of 1912 Powhatan St.
  2. The number will reply, ask any clarifying questions, and give you a list of any nearby 311 work.
  3. If the existing reports don't cover your need, ask the textbot to submit a request, and it will interface with the RVA311 site to do so! (Currently only supports pothole and sidewalk repairs.)
